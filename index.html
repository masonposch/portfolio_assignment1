About Me

Hi, my name is Mason Posch and this is my portfolio page for The Coding Bootcamp at UT Austin. I've lived in Austin for about a year now, having moved from Wichita, Kansas, where I went to school. After graduating with a bachelor's degree in communication and being in the workforce for a year or so doing sales and copywriting, I decided it was time for a change.

Since moving to Austin, I landed a role in graphic design, which quickly turned my interests towards user experiences and interface designs for different types of web based products. After creating my own concepts and designs, I wanted to further my knowledge on how to make these ideas functional. Five months go by of finding every online programming tutorial that I can, and I realized that development has become my key focus on a career that I would like to pursue.
